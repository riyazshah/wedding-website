National Parks
Rainier/Tahoma | Olympics | North Cascades
We live within a few hours of three amazing national parks! We love getting out and camping/hiking in them, and highly recommend making some time to explore if you can. Just be warned - campsite reservations go live 6 months in advance and book up quick! If you can be flexible, you can sometimes get lucky with mid-week campsites or last minute cancellations (in our experience).
San Juan Islands
Visit the San Juans
The San Juan Islands are a beautiful place to spend a few days away from the city, and are reachable by seaplane or ferry. Go for the hiking, camping, kayaking, sailing, whale watching, or just hanging out on rocky beaches and waving to Canada-ing. Say hi to the resident orca pod and see some stunning PNW sunsets.
